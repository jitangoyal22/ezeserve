# Design Sample Options

## Option 1: Gradient Holographic
- Background: White to light purple gradient
- Accents: Iridescent gradients (purple/blue/pink)
- Style: Soft rounded, glassmorphism, floating cards
- Vibe: Apple-like, premium, modern

## Option 2: Vibrant Sunset
- Background: Cream/beige base
- Accents: Orange, coral, warm reds
- Style: Organic curves, warm shadows
- Vibe: Energetic, appetizing, friendly

## Option 3: Ocean Tech
- Background: Light blue/cyan tones
- Accents: Teal, turquoise, deep blue
- Style: Flowing shapes, wave patterns
- Vibe: Fresh, clean, calming

## Option 4: Candy Pop
- Background: Soft pink/lavender
- Accents: Pink, purple, mint green
- Style: Playful, bubbly, rounded
- Vibe: Fun, Instagram-worthy, trendy
